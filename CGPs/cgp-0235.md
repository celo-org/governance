## Summary

* Reduce group deregister duration from 15552000 (180 days), to 604800 (1 week)
* Reduce validator deregister duration from 5184000 seconds (60 days), to 259200 (3 days)
* There will be no change to the locked gold requirements (10000000000000000000000 wei)

## Motivation

Moving into the L2, there is no need to have a 6 month unlock delay on validator and group deregistration since consensus is no longer in danger. Shorter delays will better align operational flexibility with the protocol's current risk profile and with the conclusion of the validator incentives program as signalled by CGP 218.
